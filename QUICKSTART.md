## Batch Generation

### Create Multiple QR Codes at Once

1. **Prepare CSV file** (see examples folder):
   - `example_urls.csv` - Website links
   - `example_wifi.csv` - Wi-Fi networks
   - `example_vcards.csv` - Contact cards

2. **Go to "Batch Generator" tab**

3. **Upload your CSV file**

4. **Select matching mode** (URLs/Wi-Fi/vCards)

5. **Click "Generate Batch"**

6. **Download gallery image** with all QR codes
